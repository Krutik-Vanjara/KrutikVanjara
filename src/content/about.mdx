I am a final-year M2 student in **IoT & Cybersecurity** at ESILV Graduate School of Engineering, Paris — with a foundational background in Physics and Electronics from St. Xavier's College, Ahmedabad, India.

My work sits at the intersection of **industrial IoT**, **OT/ICS security**, and **embedded systems engineering**. I build things that run on constrained hardware, move data reliably across industrial networks, and stay secure in adversarial environments.

## What I do

**At BlackFox Partners** — I built a multi-threaded Python CLI platform (70 parallel tasks) that maps local hardware nodes, ingests raw socket packet logs, and cuts diagnostic time by 65%. Field teams use it to turn fragmented monitoring logs into actionable views.

**At ESILV — MicroEJ Partnership** — Served as Technical Lead configuring MicroEJ VEE microcontrollers with BLE and RFID sensors, routing real-time telemetry into ThingsBoard dashboards via REST APIs.

**Renault × Capgemini Hackathon** — Audited aerospace PLM data lifecycles and designed KPI scorecards for an industrial jury panel. 3rd place out of 500+ participants.
